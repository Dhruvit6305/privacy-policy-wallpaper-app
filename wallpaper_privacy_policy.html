Wallpaper App Privacy

Overview

This wallpaper app is developed and maintained by Dhruvit Suthar. The sole purpose of this app is to provide users with high-quality wallpapers organized by category and orientation, ensuring a great browsing experience.

Information We Collect

No user data is collected. This app does not collect or share any personal or sensitive information. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions strictly for functional use:

  • Set Wallpaper: Required to allow users to set wallpapers directly from within the app.
  • Storage Access: Needed to download wallpapers to the device and to display them in the device's gallery.
  • Media Scanner: Used to run a search or media scan function to ensure that downloaded wallpapers appear in the device gallery.
These permissions are only used to deliver the features of the app. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored by the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
